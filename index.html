Model Making

Bring your structural visions to life

Date 30 April 2026
Time 10:00 AM to 1:00 PM

Rules and Regulations

  1. Participation is in teams of maximum 3 members.
  2. 50% of the model must be prepared at home and brought to the venue.
  3. Remaining 50% must be completed at the venue within the allotted time.
  4. Only waste/recyclable materials may be used.
  5. Participants must bring their own materials and tools.
  6. Use of hazardous materials is strictly prohibited.
  7. The model must be original and created only by team members.
  8. Judging criteria: creativity, use of waste materials, teamwork, and presentation.
  9. Maintain cleanliness and follow organizers’ instructions.
  10. Judges’ decision will be final.
  11. Rule violations may lead to disqualification.
