Documentation

Showcase your research and presentation skills

Date April 28, 2026
Time 11:00 AM to 12:00 PM

Rules and Regulations

  1. All participants should register their names before the event.
  2. Participants must report to the venue 15 minutes before the scheduled time.
  3. The documentation topic should be relevant, creative, and original.
  4. The documentation video should exceed 3 minutes and must be clear, creative, and well-presented.
  5. The voice-over for the documentation video must be in English only.
  6. Any copied content from books, internet, or other sources will not be accepted.
  7. Participants should maintain discipline and follow the instructions given by the coordinators.
  8. Judges' decision will be final.
